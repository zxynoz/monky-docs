# Troubleshooting

Having issues with your M.O.N.K.Y wallet? Here are solutions to common problems.

## Connection Issues

### Wallet Won't Connect

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Check Internet Connection">
    Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Refresh Browser">
    Try refreshing the page or restarting your browser
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Clear Cache">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Try Different RPC">
    Switch to a different Solana RPC endpoint
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Transaction Issues

### Transaction Stuck

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Network">
    Verify Solana network status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    Transactions can take 30-60 seconds during congestion
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Increase Priority Fee">
    Try resending with a higher priority fee
  </Step>
</Steps>
