# Your First Transaction

Ready to send your first transaction on Solana? This guide will walk you through the process step-by-step using M.O.N.K.Y wallet.

### What You'll Need

- Recipient's Solana wallet address
- Amount you want to send
- Small amount of SOL for network fees

## Sending Your First Transaction

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open M.O.N.K.Y Wallet">
    Launch your M.O.N.K.Y wallet and ensure you're connected
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Send">
    Navigate to the Send section in your wallet interface
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Token">
    Choose SOL or any SPL token you want to send
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Recipient">
    Paste or type the recipient's wallet address
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Amount">
    Specify how much you want to send
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Details">
    Double-check recipient address and amount
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Sign Transaction">
    Confirm and sign the transaction
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Transaction Complete">
    Your transaction is now processing on Solana!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Safety First

<Warning>
**Always double-check the recipient address before sending! Solana transactions are irreversible.**
</Warning>

<Tip>
**Start Small:** For your first transaction, send a small test amount to familiarize yourself with the process.
</Tip>
