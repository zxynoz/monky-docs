### Password Security

<Steps>
  <Step title="Strong Password">
    Use minimum 12 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Unique Password">
    Never reuse your wallet password elsewhere
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Password Manager">
    Use a reputable password manager
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Regular Updates">
    Change your password periodically
  </Step>
</Steps>
