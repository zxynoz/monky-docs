## Best Practices

### Portfolio Management Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="📊 Regular Review">
    **Consistent Monitoring**
    
    • Review portfolio allocation monthly
    • Assess performance against goals
    • Rebalance based on risk tolerance
    • Update investment thesis regularly
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="🎯 Diversification">
    **Risk Management**
    
    • Avoid over-concentration in single assets
    • Diversify across sectors and market caps
    • Consider correlation between holdings
    • Balance high-risk/high-reward positions
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="💰 Cost Management">
    **Fee Optimization**
    
    • Monitor and minimize transaction fees
    • Use batch transactions when possible
    • Consider tax implications of trades
    • Optimize for long-term holding
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="📈 Long-term Focus">
    **Strategic Approach**
    
    • Focus on long-term trends over short-term noise
    • Dollar-cost average into positions
    • Avoid emotional trading decisions
    • Stick to investment strategy
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
