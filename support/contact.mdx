### üîê Security Issues

**Priority: URGENT**

If you suspect unauthorized access to your wallet or notice suspicious activity:

1. **Immediately** change your wallet password
2. Move funds to a secure wallet
3. Contact us via **Telegram** or **Email** immediately
4. Do not share sensitive information

**Priority: HIGH**

‚Ä¢ Include transaction signature
‚Ä¢ Specify the exact error message
